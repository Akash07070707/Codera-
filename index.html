Artificial Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence refers to the intelligence of machines. Unlike the natural intelligence possessed by humans and animals, AI enables machines to perform functions such as learning, planning, reasoning, and problem-solving. It involves simulating human intelligence in machines, making it one of the fastest-growing developments in the world of technology and innovation. AI has the potential to address major challenges and crisis situations .

Machine Learning

Machine Learning is a subfield of Artificial Intelligence (AI) that focuses on developing systems capable of learning from data without explicit programming. Unlike traditional computational approaches, where algorithms follow explicit instructions, ML allows computers to train on data inputs and use statistical analysis to generate output values within specific ranges. The goal of ML is to understand data structures and fit that data into models that can be understood and utilized by humans.
